CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Maria Clara
  • A woman of high social standing, she is thought to be the daughter of Capitan Tiago and goddaughter of Father Dámaso.
  • Maria Clara was a sweet and kindhearted young woman. Raised with affection, Maria Clara was well-loved by everyone around her.
Sisa
  • Sisa's most prominent trait is her deep and unwavering love for her children, Basilio and Crispín. She is a devoted mother who cares for her sons intensely.
  • Sisa's love for her children is her driving force, and she is willing to endure immense pain and suffering to protect and care for them.
Pilosopo Tasyo
  • An old man who previously studied philosophy and is believed to be crazy by most of the community.
  • He respects Ibarra and gives him valuable advice, and also helped Ibarra's father before him.
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • A wealthy young mestizo who has just returned to the Philippines after seven years of studying in Europe.
  • Ibarra is sophisticated, highly esteemed, and very idealistic.
Elias
  • Elias was a selfless man bound by a code of honor. Growing up wealthy, he was initially an arrogant individual; his quarreling with a relative and beating up his disguised father was emblematic of this.
  • After losing everything and learning the true history of his family, Elias dedicated himself to helping those also oppressed by society.
